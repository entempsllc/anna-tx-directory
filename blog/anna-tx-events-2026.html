Complete Guide to Events in Anna, TX - 2026

← Back to Blog

Anna, Texas is a community that knows how to celebrate! From Independence Day fireworks to school events, there's always something happening in Anna. Here's your complete 2026 events calendar.

June 2026

June 14, 2026 - Anna ISD End-of-Year Celebration at Coyote Stadium. Free admission.

July 2026

July 4, 2026 - Anna 4th of July Fireworks at Slayter Creek Park. Annual Independence Day celebration with fireworks at dusk.

August 2026

August 12, 2026 - Back-to-School Bash. Anna ISD community event with school supply giveaways, food trucks, and live music.

October 2026

October 18, 2026 - Anna Fall Festival at Downtown Square. Pumpkin patch, food vendors, local artisans. Family friendly!

December 2026

December 6, 2026 - Anna Christmas on the Square. Holiday lighting ceremony, Santa visits, local vendors.

For a full calendar, visit annatexas.gov/events or the Greater Anna Chamber of Commerce.
